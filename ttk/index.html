Project Description

Taman Teluk Kumbar is a low cost appartnment project which consists of 620 units of low cost appartnment and 55 unit of shoplot spanning across 7.7 acers of land situated along the beach and it is only about 5km from penang airport.

The project was completed in 1996 at the gross evaluation of 18.8 million RM.
